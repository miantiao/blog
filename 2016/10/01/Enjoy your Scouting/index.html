Enjoy your Scouting

发表于 2016-10-01   |   分类于 译文

Getting outdoors and experiencing the scenery, sun and sea is a universal pleasure enjoyed by millions of people on the planet. But for it to be the pleasure we expect, we need to be trained so that it doesn’t become a battle for survival.

The Skill Sheets have been compiled as a training aid that will allow all Scouts in New Zealand to:

  • safely experience adventure and enjoy fun in the outdoors,

  • develop the skills needed to survive in life, particularly in emergencies,

  • care for the environment in which we live and pass it on in good condition to the next generation.

These Scout Skill Sheets are a ‘one stop shop’ of basic information for the members of SCOUTS and their leaders.

If you follow the guidelines, you will safely visit and enjoy the magnificent scenery that most people see only on TV, the internet, calendars and in magazines. You will also enjoy the friendship of many others as you live your life.
Enjoy your Scouting.

到户外去，体验大自然、阳光和海洋，这是这个星球上的大多数人都会沉浸其中的事情。然而，为了更好的享受这种沉浸，我们有必要为之进行训练，否则，预期中的享受会变成求存的挣扎。

为此，我们综合整理出了这份能力清单，它将作为一个训练指引，帮助所有新西兰的童子军成员达成以下目标：

  • 在户外安全地体验冒险并乐在其中
  • 针对野外生存，尤其是突发紧急情况，培养技能
  • 保护好我们周边的环境，并使之良性地世代相传

这些技能对童子军成员和他们的领队而言，是一整套“一站式”的训练内容。
如果你遵循这个指导手册，你将能够安全地探访和享受那些大多数其他人只能在电视、网络、明信片和杂志上才能看到的壮丽奇景。
而在这个过程中，你还将会和众多与你生活方式相似的人成为朋友，同乐于斯。

祝你们“军旅快乐”

​ ——译自《新西兰童子军活动手册》前言
